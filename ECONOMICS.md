# Unit Economics: Lumina AI Audit

Lumina is designed as a **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) reduction engine** for Credex. By providing upfront value through an audit, we build the trust required to close high-ticket infrastructure credit deals.

## 1. The Value of a Converted Lead
For Credex, a "converted lead" is a startup that purchases a discounted credit package (OpenAI, Anthropic, AWS, etc.). 
- **Average Deal Size**: $50,000 (e.g., a startup buying $100k worth of Claude credits for a upfront cost of $70k).
- **Credex Margin**: Typically ~10-15% of the transaction value. This results in a **Net Revenue of $5,000 per deal**.
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Startups that buy credits once are highly likely to renew or diversify their stack (e.g., moving from OpenAI to Anthropic credits). We estimate a 3-year LTV of **$15,000 - $20,000** per retained customer.

## 2. CAC at Each Channel (Audit-Based)
Traditional B2B lead gen for infrastructure is expensive ($500+ per qualified lead). Lumina drives this down:
- **Organic Social (X/LinkedIn)**: Direct cost is $0, with an estimated "effort cost" of $2,000/mo for content curation. If this drives 200 audits/mo, the **CAC per Audit is $10**.
- **Paid Search (e.g., "Reduce OpenAI Bill")**: We estimate a $10 - $15 Cost Per Click (CPC). With a conservative 10% audit completion rate, the **Paid CAC per Audit is $100 - $150**.
- **The "Viral Lift"**: Because each audit has a shareable URL, every paid user can potentially bring in 0.5 organic users, effectively reducing the blended CAC by 33%.

## 3. The Conversion Funnel Math (The $1M ARR Path)
To reach a target of **$1M in annual net margin**, Credex needs to close approximately 200 credit deals per year (~17 deals per month).

**The Monthly Funnel:**
1. **Top of Funnel (Visitors)**: 10,000 unique visitors (via GTM channels).
2. **Audit Completion (20%)**: 2,000 completed audits.
3. **Lead Capture (40%)**: 800 emails/leads captured.
4. **Qualified Leads (> $500 savings, 10%)**: 80 high-value leads.
5. **Consultation Booked (25%)**: 20 booked calls.
6. **Deals Closed (20% of calls)**: 4 deals per month.

*Self-Correction*: 4 deals per month at $5k margin is $240k ARR. To reach **$1M ARR**, we need to either 4x the traffic (40k visitors) or improve the conversion rate from "Lead Captured" to "Consultation" by offering more direct incentives (e.g., "Get an extra 5% off your first Credex deal by completing this audit").

## 4. Profitability & ROI
The operational costs of Lumina are negligible:
- **Hosting (Vercel)**: $20/mo
- **Database (Supabase)**: $25/mo
- **Email (Resend)**: $20/mo
- **AI Summary (Smart Mock)**: $0/mo (since we pivoted away from live API calls for the MVP).

**Total OpEx**: ~$65/mo. 
**Break-even Point**: A single deal ($5,000 margin) covers the entire annual operating cost of the tool 6x over. This represents an incredible **ROI for a lead-generation asset**.

## 5. What must be true for $1M ARR?
For this tool to drive $1M in margin within 18 months:
- It must become the **"Standard Benchmark"** for AI spend. Founders should ask each other "What was your Lumina score?" during budgeting sessions.
- We must achieve a **K-factor of >0.5**, meaning the tool grows organically through the shareable links.
- Credex must have a diverse enough inventory of credits to "fulfill" the savings identified in the audits across OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google.
