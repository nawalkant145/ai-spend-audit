# Unit Economics: Lumina AI Audit

Lumina is a **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) reduction engine** for Credex.

## 1. Value of a Converted Lead
A "converted lead" for Credex is a startup that buys discounted credits.
- **Average Credit Deal**: $50,000 (e.g., $100k of Claude credits for $70k).
- **Credex Margin**: ~10% ($5,000 net revenue per deal).
- **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: If a startup renews or buys different credits (e.g., switching from OpenAI to Anthropic), LTV grows to **$15,000+**.

## 2. CAC at Each Channel
- **Organic Social (X/LinkedIn)**: $0 direct cost + $2,000/mo in content effort.
- **Paid Search (e.g., "OpenAI bill too high")**: ~$15 per click. With a 10% audit completion rate, **Audit CAC = $150**.
- **Cold Outreach (BDR)**: ~$500 per qualified meeting.

## 3. Conversion Funnel Math
To be profitable, we need these ratios:
- **Landing Page → Audit Completed**: 20%
- **Audit Completed → Lead Captured (Email)**: 40%
- **Lead Captured → Credex Consultation**: 5% (High-savings cases only)
- **Consultation → Credit Purchase**: 20%

**The Math**:
1,000 Visitors → 200 Audits → 80 Leads → 4 Consultations → **0.8 Deals**.
Cost per Deal (at $150/audit) = $37,500. 
*Self-Correction*: This is high. We must rely on **Organic/Viral** channels (the "Shareable URL") to bring the Audit CAC down to <$20.

## 4. Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To drive $1M in net margin (revenue), Credex needs ~200 credit deals/year.
- **Target**: 17 deals/month.
- **Requirement**: ~1,700 leads/month → **~4,000 audits/month**.
- **Reality Check**: This is achievable if the tool becomes the "standard" for AI tool benchmarking (the "Lighthouse for AI Spend").

## 5. Investment vs. Return
- **Build Cost**: ~1 week of engineering/design.
- **Monthly OpEx**: ~$100 (Anthropic API, Supabase, Hosting).
- **Breakeven**: 1 single deal covers the entire annual operating cost of the tool 50x over.
