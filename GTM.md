# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy: Lumina AI Audit

## 1. Target User
The exact target user is a **Seed to Series B Tech Founder** or an **Engineering Manager (EM)** at a startup with 10–100 employees. 
- **The Trigger**: They just noticed their OpenAI or Anthropic bill crossed $2,000/mo and realized they have no idea if that's "normal."
- **Company Stage**: Scaling companies that are shifting from "growth at all costs" to "efficient growth."

## 2. Intent-Based Search & Scroll
What they are doing right before they want this tool:
- Googling "Cursor Pro vs Business for teams"
- Scrolling through Reddit threads like `r/startups` asking "What is your team's AI spend per dev?"
- Reading "How to reduce OpenAI API costs" blog posts.
- Checking their AWS/Google Cloud bill and seeing the "AI Services" line item spike.

## 3. High-Density Hangouts
- **Twitter (X)**: "AI Twitter" and "Builder Twitter." Lists like "Top AI Founders."
- **Slack/Discord**: Founder-led communities like *Launch House*, *Y Combinator (Bookface)*, and *Indie Hackers*.
- **Subreddits**: `r/startups`, `r/SaaS`, `r/OpenAI`.

## 4. The "Zero Dollar" First 100 Users
1. **The "Programmatic SEO" Play on X**: Search for people complaining about AI bills or asking about tool comparisons. Reply with a screenshot of a Lumina audit and a link. "Hey, built this to solve exactly that. Here's what an optimized stack looks like."
2. **Community Value-Add**: Post a "State of AI Spend 2026" report on Hacker News/LinkedIn using anonymized data from our first few audits. Provide the tool as the "how to check your own" link.
3. **Direct DM to Portfolio Companies**: Leverage the existing Credex network to audit their current portfolio.

## 5. The Unfair Distribution Channel
**Credex's existing partnerships**. Credex already has relationships with companies looking to sell/offload credits. We can offer a "Credit Match" feature where the audit doesn't just show savings, it shows the *exact* credit package available on Credex that fits their audited needs.

## 6. Week 1 Traction Goals
- **Total Audits**: 250
- **Lead Capture Rate**: 40% (100 emails)
- **High-Savings Consultations Booked**: 10
- **Social Shares**: 15 (Public URLs shared on X/LinkedIn)
